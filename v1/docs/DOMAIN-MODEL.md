# Domain model

## Core terms

- **Concept:** curriculum-independent knowledge, skill, method, representation, or misconception.
- **Curriculum Requirement:** describes expected depth and timing for one or more Concepts.
- **Resource:** reusable explanatory or supporting material.
- **Exercise:** reusable task requiring student action or response.
- **Activity:** lesson-context execution of Resources or Exercises.
- **Teaching Profile:** reusable pedagogical preferences.
- **Lesson Blueprint:** ordered lesson sections and timing expectations.
- **Lesson Layout:** printable or projected visual structure.
- **Annual Plan:** classroom-specific school-year plan.
- **Phase:** related sequence of lessons.
- **Lesson:** concrete planned or completed classroom event.
- **Lesson Run:** actual execution record.
- **Learning Evidence:** answer, explanation, correction, confidence, observation, or demonstration.
- **Concept State:** explainable overview derived from evidence, not a canonical mutable mastery score.

## Revision semantics

Draft saves are mutable. Publishing creates an identifiable revision. Lessons pin exact content revisions. Compatibility is classified as presentation-only, content-equivalent, planning-impacting, or contract-breaking. The teacher may override the suggested class.

## Relations

Relations are registered, typed, and directed unless their contract says otherwise. Numeric dimensions have relation-specific meaning. `covers.contribution` is not interchangeable with `alternative-to.similarity`.
