# Fonat brand

The mark is a subtle F formed by three rounded interwoven strands. The pumpkin accent remains in the vertical stem, the neutral strand forms the upper arm, and the blue primary strand forms the lower arm. Keep the crossings clear, retain generous negative space, and do not redraw it as a plant, road junction, K, book, brain, or spool.

Use `fonat-mark.svg` at compact sizes, `fonat-logo.svg` for ordinary branded surfaces, and the monochrome version for print and single-color contexts.
