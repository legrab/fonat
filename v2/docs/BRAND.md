# Fonat brand direction

## Motto

Primary motto:

> Szálról szálra.  
> Thread by thread.

Separate descriptive line for About and README contexts:

> Az oktatás minden szála egy helyen.  
> Every thread of teaching in one place.

Do not place both lines immediately adjacent in compact product chrome.

## Mark

The Fonat mark is built from three rounded strands rising from the bottom as a compact braid:

- the accent strand remains in the vertical stem and continues to the top,
- the neutral strand branches right near the top to form the upper horizontal stroke,
- the primary strand branches right at mid-height to form the lower horizontal stroke,
- the result subtly suggests an `F` without becoming a conventional outlined letter.

Use two or three legible crossings, rounded terminals, and generous negative space. Reject variants that resemble a plant, road interchange, letter K, literal textile spool, book, brain, graduation cap, atom, or lightbulb.

Source SVGs live in `apps/web/public/assets/brand`. They must remain legible in monochrome, 16 to 32 pixel favicons, light and dark themes, print, and projected presentation.
