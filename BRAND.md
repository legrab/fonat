# Fonat brand

The mark is three rounded interwoven strands forming a compact, subtle `F`. The accent strand stays vertical, the neutral strand forms the upper stroke, and the primary strand forms the lower stroke. Use the monochrome source at small sizes and in print.

Primary motto: **Szálról szálra. / Thread by thread.**

About line: **Az oktatás minden szála egy helyen. / Every thread of teaching in one place.**

Do not redraw the mark as a plant, book, brain, road interchange, lightbulb, atom, graduation cap, or textile spool.
